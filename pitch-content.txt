SLIDE 4: PRODUCT ARCHITECTURE
Four integrated systems that compound into sustained execution velocity:
1. INVITE-ONLY PITCH ROOMS
Curated rooms where handpicked builders pitch live to operators, mentors, and accelerator partners. Only builders with active projects and execution proof allowed. Top teams get direct pipeline into accelerators and execution programs. This creates a talent discovery engine for early-stage capital.
2. STREAK-BASED BUILDING
Builders set public goals with self-assigned deadlines. They must post daily progress to maintain streaks. Streaks are visible to peers and mentors. Breaking streaks has social cost. Maintaining streaks builds credibility and accelerator deal flow.
3. HOUSES SYSTEM
On signup, builders take a sorting quiz based on work ethic, building style, collaboration preferences, and execution tempo. Users are sorted into houses with similar builders. Houses compete on streak completions, shipped projects, and hackathon wins. This creates belonging, identity, and intrinsic motivation.
4. AI TEAM MATCHING
Builders swipe on projects, skills, and working styles. AI matches complementary skill sets and execution patterns. Reduces founder friction and early team churn. Optimizes for compatibility, not popularity.
